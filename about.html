Our Vision

At NextStepCoders, Our vision is to develop in every learner the ability to think critically about any given problem statement and design effective, practical solutions. Our goal is to help each candidate be confident, competent, and professionally presented to meet landers greater challenge of modern technology.

Our Mission

We want to develop curiosity and confidence in the next generation of technologists through conceptual clarity and the practical application of knowledge. We want to create purposeful learning experiences in real-time, learning contexts that help students learn how to not only code but learn how to communicate, collaborate, and create. In our courses – Basic Python to Machine Learning – we hope that individuals develop as independent thinkers, real-world problem solvers, and with confidence and professionalism.
